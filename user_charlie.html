“Ah, my pretty little cows!” cried Mr Wonka. “How I love those cows!” “But why can’t we see them?” asked Veruca Salt. “Why do we have to go rushing past all these lovely rooms?” “We shall stop in time!” called out Mr Wonka. “Don’t be so madly impatient!”

FIZZY LIFTING DRINKS, it said on the next door. “Oh, those are fabulous!” cried Mr Wonka. “They fill you with bubbles, and the bubbles are full of a special kind of gas, and this gas is so terrifically lifting that it lifts you right off the ground just like a balloon, and up you go until your head hits the ceiling – and there you stay.” “But how do you come down again?”
